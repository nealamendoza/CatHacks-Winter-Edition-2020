What is SafeSpace?

SafeSpace is a platform that checks if a website is suitable for children. It scans a webpage to see if it contains any vulgar or innapropriate language.

Who is SafeSpace used for?

SafeSpace is perfect for parents who want to keep their children safe. It is highly recommended to use SafeSpace to avoid exposing the younger audience to bad influences on the internet. SafeSpace is also a great tool for schools. Schools can use our platform to figure out which sites need to be blocked, and which sites are safe for students to see. Students may stumble across articles or webpages containing profanity, and it is the school's job to make sure to censor the right webpages.

Why should you use SafeSpace?

With the vast use of the World Wide Web, parents and
guardians should be aware of the sites their children are using.
Many webpages aren't suitable for the younger
audience, and it is easy for them to stumble upon a site that
